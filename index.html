How the AI coach actually works

Not a "smart" template engine. A genuinely adaptive intelligence that learns your body's patterns and recalibrates every single day.

01 / Deep biometric intake

Connect your wearable — Apple Watch, Garmin, Oura Ring, Whoop or any device with open API. The AI ingests your resting heart rate, HRV score, sleep stages, recovery index and active calories in real time. No manual logging. No effort on your part.

02 / Daily mood and energy scan

Each morning you get a 15‑second check‑in: three taps, no journaling required. Combined with your overnight biometric data, the AI builds a precise picture of your actual capacity for the day — not what the program says you should do.

03 / Adaptive plan generation

Based on your data, the AI generates today's session: exact exercises, duration, intensity zones, rest periods and pace. High-energy day? It pushes. Recovering from social exhaustion and poor sleep? It scales back and adds restorative movement without guilt.
